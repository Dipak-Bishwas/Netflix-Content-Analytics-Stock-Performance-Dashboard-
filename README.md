# Netflix Content & Stock Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze Netflix content trends, audience engagement, and Netflix stock market performance. The dashboard combines entertainment datasets with financial data to generate business insights through data visualization and analytics.

---

## Project Objectives
- Analyze Netflix movies and TV shows by genre, country, release year, and ratings.
- Study audience engagement using IMDb and TMDB scores.
- Explore actor and cast participation using credits data.
- Track Netflix stock price movements and market trends.
- Create interactive dashboards for business intelligence and decision-making.

---

## Datasets Used

### 1. Netflix Titles Dataset
Contains:
- Movies & TV Shows
- Genre
- Runtime
- Release Year
- IMDb & TMDB Ratings
- Popularity Scores

### 2. Credits Dataset
Contains:
- Actor Names
- Character Roles
- Cast Participation
- Movie/Show Relationships

### 3. Netflix Stock Dataset
Contains:
- Open Price
- Close Price
- High & Low Price
- Volume
- Historical Stock Performance

## Dashboard Insights
- Movies dominate Netflix content compared to TV Shows.
- Drama and Comedy are the most popular genres.
- The United States contributes the highest number of titles.
- Highly rated content generally shows higher popularity scores.
- Netflix stock prices show significant volatility over time.
